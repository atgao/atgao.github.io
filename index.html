CSE Ph.D. student at the University of Washington in WildLab, advised by Katharina Reinecke. Broadly, my research focuses on understanding the different uses of digital technologies between users of varying backgrounds and how to build customized tools for them. Currently, my focuses are (1) on creating adaptive and personalized UI interfaces (2) understanding how different value misalignments with LLMs affects collaborative downstream tasks to create more responsible AI.
